Quick answers to the most common questions.

Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take? +

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. Express shipping (1-2 business days) is available for an additional fee. International shipping varies by destination, usually 7-14 business days.

How can I track my order? +

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" section. Click on any order to see real-time tracking information.

Do you offer free shipping? +

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $35. Members of our loyalty program enjoy free shipping on all orders regardless of the total amount.

Can I change my shipping address after placing an order? +

If your order hasn't shipped yet, you can change the shipping address by contacting our customer service team immediately. Once an order has shipped, the address cannot be changed, but you may be able to redirect the package through the carrier.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy? +

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Books must be in their original condition—unread, unmarked, and with no damage. Simply initiate a return through your account or contact customer service for a prepaid return label.

How long do refunds take to process? +

Once we receive your return, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. The refund will be credited to your original payment method. Please allow an additional 5-10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement, depending on your bank.

What if I received a damaged book? +

We're sorry to hear that! Please contact us within 7 days of delivery with photos of the damage. We'll send you a replacement at no additional cost or issue a full refund—your choice. You won't need to return the damaged item.

Payment & Gift Cards

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Booking Journey gift cards. We also offer financing options through Affirm for orders over $50.

How do I use a gift card or promo code? +

During checkout, you'll see a field labeled "Gift Card or Promo Code." Enter your code there and click "Apply." The discount will be reflected in your order total. Note that only one promo code can be used per order, but gift cards can be combined.

Do gift cards expire? +

No, Booking Journey gift cards never expire and have no fees. The full value remains available until used. You can check your gift card balance anytime by logging into your account or contacting customer service.

Account & Membership

How do I create an account? +

Click the "Sign Up" button at the top of any page. You'll need to provide your email address and create a password. You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account for faster registration. Account creation is free!

I forgot my password. How do I reset it? +

Click "Sign In" and then "Forgot Password." Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. The link expires after 24 hours for security. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

What are the benefits of creating an account? +

Account holders enjoy faster checkout, order tracking, wishlist creation, personalized recommendations, exclusive member discounts, early access to sales, and the ability to earn loyalty points on every purchase.
